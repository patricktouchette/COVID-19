Coronavirus COVID-19 | New Cases Per Day

Are we flattening the curve? Here is a data visualization of the number of new cases per day. You can filter by country/province/region. The order is by country. You can hover your mouse over the points to see more information. Please note, some countries have data by province/region: China, Canada, Australia and more.


Data source: The excellent github repository by Johns Hopkins CSSE https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19.
